Privacy Policy for The GovLab AIEP Project

How We Handle Your Individualized Education Program (IEP) Documents

  • When you upload an IEP PDF, we immediately create a text version using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and delete the original PDF right away.
  • We do not store any PDFs of IEPs.
  • Before anything is processed further, we run the text through a tool from AWS that removes personal information (like addresses, social security numbers and phone numbers) — though we keep names in the document to make it useful. (Using AWS Comprehend)
  • Only this cleaned-up version of the IEP is sent to our language models for processing.

The Language Models We Use

  • We use Mistral’s enterprise version and the OpenAI API for language processing.
  • Neither company trains their AI models using the documents we send them. Your data stays private and is not reused for any future AI training.

How We Keep Everything Secure

Access and Identity Protection

  • Role-based access control and multi-factor authentication: Only the right people can access systems, and they must verify their identity.
  • Encryption: Your data is protected both when it's being sent between services and when it's stored.
  • Audit logging: We keep detailed logs to track access and actions for security and accountability.

Safe Infrastructure

  • Infrastructure as Code: Our systems are built in a repeatable, reviewable way.
  • Separate environments: We keep development and live environments separate to prevent accidental leaks.
  • Secure updates: All code changes go through automated testing and tracking before being deployed.

Monitoring and Response

  • Real-time monitoring: We watch for security threats and can act quickly.
  • Incident response plan: If something happens, we have a clear process to investigate and fix it.

Finding and Fixing Problems Early

  • Automated security scans: We regularly scan our systems for vulnerabilities.
  • Secrets management: Passwords and sensitive configuration details are securely stored and protected.

Extra Steps for Handling Sensitive Information

  • Least-privilege access: Only people who absolutely need to see PHI can access it.

How We Handle Phone Numbers (SMS Consent & Use)

  • We only ask for your phone number if you choose to create an account using your phone instead of an email address.
  • We do not use your phone number for any purpose other than sending you One-Time Passcodes (OTPs) to verify your identity.
  • We never share or sell phone numbers, and we do not use them for marketing or communication outside of login verification.
  • Phone numbers are securely stored and encrypted, just like all other sensitive information.
  • You will only receive one SMS per login attempt.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You may request that we delete your data at any time.
  • You may opt out of SMS by replying STOP to any message. For assistance, reply HELP or contact us at info@thegovlab.org.

Contact

  • Email: info@thegovlab.org
